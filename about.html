About Us

Founded in 2020, HQL Services Liberia has set the benchmark for excellence in flight catering and deep cleaning services. Our mission is simple: provide airlines with the highest standards of safety, comfort, and cleanliness. We are committed to delivering top-notch services that ensure customer satisfaction and uphold the highest hygiene standards across all operations.

Vision: To become the leading provider of flight catering and deep cleaning services in Liberia and across Africa, ensuring a safe and comfortable experience for all passengers.

Mission: Our mission is to deliver exceptional service through reliability, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Promise to You

At HQL Services Liberia, we understand the importance of quality and consistency. We aim to foster long-lasting relationships with our clients, offering solutions tailored to their unique needs. Our team is dedicated to continuous improvement, staying ahead of industry standards and delivering services that go beyond expectations. With our expert team, modern technology, and a passion for excellence, we ensure your passengers’ safety and comfort are our top priority.
